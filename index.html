Our Flagship Devices

Engineered for accuracy, accessibility, and ease of use in diverse clinical settings.

Cervi-Lite

A portable, smartphone-based adapter for rapid and non-invasive cervical cancer screening. Its calibrated optics and AI-driven analysis deliver results in minutes, ideal for primary care and outreach programs.

OralScope

A compact intraoral fluorescence scope designed for the early detection of oral lesions. It provides high-contrast imaging to help clinicians distinguish between healthy and potentially malignant tissue with confidence.

How Our Screening Tool Works

Our device uses the principle of intrinsic fluorescence to analyze tissue at a cellular level. Healthy and cancerous cells emit light differently when stimulated by specific wavelengths, creating a unique “light signature.”

The core of our device is a common smartphone, which captures this signature through a proprietary optical attachment. Our on-board AI algorithms then process this data in real-time, providing instant, actionable results without needing a lab.
